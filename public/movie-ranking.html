The Best Movies According to Laura

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Spirit: Stallion of the Cimarron

This is my favourite animated movie. I love the beautiful images and message that it conveys.

Lemony Snicket's A Series of Unfortunate Events

Really cool and interesting movie, it has you wondering what else could go wrong the whole movie. Also the message about family is amazing.

Taken

Very interesting action movie, with an amazing story and effects.
